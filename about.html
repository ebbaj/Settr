ABOUT SETTR.

SETTR is a collaboration platform designed to connect customers, influencers and brands.

Our company's platform offers webshops and customized profiles of social influencers containing products they wish to endorse, enabling companies to advertise for their products and reach out to more consumers in a cost-effective manner.
